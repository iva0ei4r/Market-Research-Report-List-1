<p><p>LCP soft boards are categorized into three main market types based on their construction: single layer, dual layer, and multi-layer. Single layer boards consist of one standard dielectric layer, offering basic functionality for simpler applications. Dual layer boards incorporate two dielectric layers, providing enhanced performance and signal integrity for more complex designs. Multi-layer boards consist of three or more layers, enabling intricate circuitry and improved electrical performance, making them ideal for high-density applications in advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
