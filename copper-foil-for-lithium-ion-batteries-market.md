<p><p>The copper foil market for lithium-ion batteries primarily comprises two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electroplating process, offering high purity and excellent conductivity, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, rolled copper foil is created by mechanically rolling copper to desired thickness, providing versatility in manufacturing and lower costs. Both types are essential for battery anodes, influencing overall battery efficiency, energy density, and lifecycle performance.</p></p>
