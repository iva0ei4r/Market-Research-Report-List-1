<p><ul><li>Qualitative Filtration Papers</li><li>Quantitative Filtration Papers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filtration papers market is categorized into two main types: qualitative and quantitative filtration papers. Qualitative filtration papers are designed for indicator purposes, providing visual results in laboratory settings. They typically separate components based on particle size and are used for routine analyses. In contrast, quantitative filtration papers are engineered for precise measurement, ensuring accurate weight and concentration of filtered substances. These papers are often used in more detailed scientific research, where reproducibility and precision are paramount.</p></p>
