<p><p>The metal working lubricants market encompasses three primary types: quenching, cutting and grinding, and corrosion preventives. Quenching lubricants are used in heat treatment processes to cool and harden metals efficiently, enhancing their strength and durability. Cutting and grinding lubricants facilitate smooth machining by reducing friction, dissipating heat, and improving surface finish during metal removal processes. Corrosion preventives protect metal surfaces from oxidation and rust, extending the lifespan of tools and components in various industrial applications.</p></p>
